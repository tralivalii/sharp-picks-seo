Football Betting: Analyze Matches Like a Professional

Beyond the Scoreline

Professional football analysis goes far beyond looking at win-loss records. Here's what actually matters.

Expected Goals (xG)

xG measures the quality of scoring chances. A team with 2.5 xG vs 0.8 xG dominated the game regardless of the actual score. xG is more predictive than actual goals for future performance.

Key Metrics to Track

  • xG (Expected Goals) — shot quality
  • Possession — but only in the final third
  • Shots on target — actual threats created
  • Defensive actions — tackles, interceptions, clearances
  • Set piece efficiency

Home Advantage

Home teams win approximately 45% of matches across Europe's top leagues. This drops to 28% for away wins, with 27% draws. Betting on a home favorite is fundamentally sound.

Our Football Methodology

We focus on elite European leagues where data is abundant: Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A, Ligue 1. These leagues offer the most predictable outcomes due to quality differentials.
