The Daily Betting Routine: How We Prepare Our Picks

Our Daily Process

Every morning, our system goes through a rigorous analytical pipeline before posting any pick.

Step 1: Data Ingestion

We pull odds from multiple APIs and bookmakers. Our system scans for matches where the favorite is priced below 1.80 and where the odds suggest value.

Step 2: Filtering

We apply our 5-point filter: odds range, home/neutral advantage, current form, head-to-head record, and injury status. Matches passing all 5 filters enter the selection pool.

Step 3: Analysis Generation

For each selected match, we generate a brief analysis explaining the reasoning. This includes key stats, recent form, and why we expect the favorite to win.

Step 4: Posting

Picks are posted to our Telegram channel with a generated bet slip image showing the match, prediction, odds, and confidence level.

Step 5: Tracking

Every pick is logged with its result. We maintain transparent statistics that you can verify yourself.
