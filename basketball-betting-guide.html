Basketball Betting Guide: Key Metrics for NBA and EuroLeague

Why Basketball is Bettor-Friendly

Basketball has the most predictable regular season outcomes of any major sport. The better team wins approximately 78% of the time — higher than football, baseball, or hockey.

Key NBA Metrics

  • Offensive Rating: Points per 100 possessions
  • Defensive Rating: Points allowed per 100 possessions
  • Net Rating: Offensive minus defensive
  • Pace: Possessions per game
  • Home/Road splits

Back-to-Back Games

Teams on the second night of a back-to-back win approximately 38% of games. This creates value opportunities when sportsbooks over-adjust and when they don't adjust enough.

Our Approach

We focus on clear talent discrepancies — top-5 teams vs bottom-10 teams. These matchups produce the most predictable outcomes and align with the Freak Bets methodology of low-odds, high-probability betting.
