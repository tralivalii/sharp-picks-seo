Tennis Betting: Why the Favorite Almost Always Wins

The Favorite's Edge in Tennis

Tennis is unique among sports: the better player wins far more consistently than the better team in team sports. Top-10 vs outside top-50: the favorite wins approximately 87% of matches.

Surface Matters

A player's ranking doesn't tell the whole story. Surface-specific ratings are more predictive:

  • Clay specialists have an edge on clay
  • Serve-dominant players thrive on grass
  • Consistent baseliners dominate hard courts

Grand Slam vs Regular Tournaments

Higher-ranked players perform better at Grand Slams (best of 5 sets) where skill differentials compound over longer matches. Regular ATP/WTA events (best of 3) are slightly more volatile.

Head-to-Head Records

A dominant H2H record is one of the strongest predictors in tennis. If Player A has beaten Player B in 6 of their last 7 meetings, that pattern tends to continue.
